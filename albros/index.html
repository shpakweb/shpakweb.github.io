About Us

Albros Shipping and Trading Company whose headquarters are based in Turkey, is a significant player in the international markets over the last 15 years. The Company operates a fleet of shallow draught dry cargo and tankers in sizes over 3,600 Company provides an agency service at Bosphorus Straits and major Turkish ports. Albros provides operational and technical management for its fleet.

The Company specializes in bulk, steel and general/project cargo (heavylift) trade. During the last 8 years Albros has managed 25 newbuildings (including 2 IMO II chemical tankers and 2 sophisticated original design Anchor Handling, Tug & Supply).The main trade is Continent, Mediterranean, Black Sea and Russian inland waters.
